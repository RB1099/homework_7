Animated generative art in multi-colour

Phil Nash

Step

Change the number of lines. The lower the number the higher the number of lines and vice versa.




Randomness

Change the amount of movement for the lines. The lower the number the lesser the movement.




Color Space

Change the way the colors move across the screen by setting their initial spacing.
